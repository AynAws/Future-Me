Career Paths

Game Development

Game developers are the people who build games, from the gameplay to the story. They wield much power in indie studios, and form the crux of any game studio.

Website Design

Website designers are those who code and design websites, typically specializing in front-end. They can, at times, work in back-end as well.

Computer Security

Cybersecurity experts develop and test anti-virus applications and other forms of digital protection. They can have knowledge in hacking to become white hat hackers, or just to know the threat they combat.

Game Development


Education

Game developers create interactive digital experiences. A degree in computer science or game design is required, with skills in programming languages like C++ or Java.
Recommended: Experience with game engines like Unity or Unreal Engine.

Impact

A story can convey strong messages. Ideas and messages can be spread through games and the stories they tell. Additionally, games can serve to rpomote certain political ideas, working for or against current politicians.

Website Design


Education

Website designers craft visually appealing and functional websites. A degree in web design, graphic design, or computer science is required, with proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
Recommended: Familiarity with UX/UI principles.

Impact

Designers can volunteer to work for charitable organizations. This can spread the organization and its message. They could also help out small business owners, giving growth to their companies through the web.

Computer Security


Education

Cybersecurity experts protect digital systems from threats. A degree in cybersecurity, computer science, or information technology is required, with knowledge in network security and programming languages.
Recommended: Certifications like CISSP or CEH.

Impact

Cybersec experts can work for government organizations and help stop domestic attacks or other forms of foreign interference. They could also help protect user privacy in large companies by keeping software up-to-date with the ability to counter new threats.
