Game Development

Game development involves creating video games for various platforms such as consoles, computers, and mobile devices. Game developers utilize programming languages, game engines, and design principles to bring games to life. They work on aspects like gameplay mechanics, graphics, audio, and storytelling.

Website Design

Website designers are responsible for creating the visual and interactive elements of websites. They use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to structure and style web pages, ensuring they are both aesthetically pleasing and functional. Website designers also consider user experience and accessibility when designing websites.

Computer Security

Computer security, or cybersecurity, involves protecting computer systems and networks from cyber threats such as malware, phishing, and hacking. Cybersecurity professionals develop and implement security measures to safeguard sensitive information and prevent unauthorized access to digital assets. They also conduct risk assessments and respond to security incidents.
